Privacy Policy

GymBRO App values your privacy.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data (such as name, email, or phone number) is collected by this app.
  • Workout logs (exercises, reps, weights) are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

Advertising

The app displays interstitial ads provided by third-party networks (e.g., Google AdMob). These ad providers may collect non-personal data, such as device identifiers, to show relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps here: Google Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The app offers an ad-free upgrade via in-app purchase. Purchases are handled securely through the Google Play Store billing system. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Sharing

No personal information is sold, shared, or transferred to us. Any data collected is handled directly by Google Play Services, Google AdMob, and other third-party services as described above.
